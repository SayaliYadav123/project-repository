Flu Disease Information

Influenza, commonly known as the flu, is a contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses. It can cause mild to severe illness and can lead to hospitalization and even death in some cases.

Symptoms

  • Fever or feeling feverish/chills
  • Cough
  • Sore throat
  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headaches
  • Fatigue (tiredness)
  • Vomiting and diarrhea (more common in children than adults)

Treatment

Most people with the flu have mild illness and do not need medical care or antiviral drugs. However, in some cases, antiviral drugs may be prescribed to treat flu illness.

Over-the-counter medications may help relieve symptoms such as fever, cough, and congestion. It is essential to stay hydrated and get plenty of rest.

Annual flu vaccination is the most effective way to prevent flu illness and its complications.
