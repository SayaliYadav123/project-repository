Feline Leukemia Information

Feline Leukemia (FeLV) is a viral disease that affects cats. It can suppress the immune system and cause various health problems.

Symptoms

  • Loss of appetite
  • Weight loss
  • Fever
  • Enlarged lymph nodes
  • Pale gums
  • Jaundice
  • Diarrhea
  • Vomiting
  • Weakness
  • Respiratory problems
  • Neurological symptoms

Treatment

There is no cure for FeLV, but supportive care can help manage symptoms and improve the quality of life for infected cats. Prevention through vaccination and avoiding exposure to infected cats is crucial.
